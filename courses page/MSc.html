Eligbility: Merit index as per C.S.J.M. University Norms. Index points will be calculated as below :

1.Markes obtained in all the theory papers in all the science subjects having practical in part I,II,III combined.

2. 50% of the marks obtained in the praticals of all subjects in part I,II,III combined.

3. In case of non-practical subjects offered at B.Sc. level 80% of the total marks shall be added in the index.

4. Markes obtained in the theory papers of the subjects in which the admission sought in part I,II,III combined.

5. 50% of the marks obtained in the pratical of all subjects in which the admission is sought in part I,II,III combined.

6. While calculating the index number of applicant coming from the institution not affiliated to C.S.J.M. university,kanpur . If the marks in the theory and practical are not mentioned seprately in the marksheets, 80% of the marks both in aggregate and in the subject concered will be taken into consideration for calculating index.
